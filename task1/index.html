Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit organization dedicated to fostering sustainable community upliftment across India.

We believe in the power of youth leadership. By engaging young volunteers, students, and professionals in structured campaigns, we construct pathways for continuous community welfare, environmental cleanup, animal relief, and primary child education.

NITI Aayog NGO Darpan 80G & 12A Certified ISO 9001:2015 Govt. Registered Sec. 8
